Set A : Instructions

*** Recommmended to be taken on Laptop/PC using a Chrome Browser, preferably in Incognito Mode.

1. Welcome to Sliding Image Puzzle Test. This test contains two sets - A & B.
Set A is COMPULSORY but Set B is OPTIONAL.

2. This is what a single game area looks like. You are given a picture divides into 9 tiles.

3. The bottom corner tile is removed and the rest tiles are shuffled. Your goal is to make moves of the tiles to get these to their original position.

4. The original image is provided as a reference on the right side of the game.

5. To make a move, click on any tile that is adjacent to the blank tile. The tile that is clikced will "slide" into the blank position.

6. Please DO NOT PRESS the BACK button, otherwise your progress will be lost and you will taken back to login page.

7. If you feel like you are stuck there is a Give-Up button on the top-right corner of the game area.
Once you finish a puzzle, the removed tile will be shown and a NEXT button will appear at the bottom of the game area. Click the button to take you to the next puzzle.

8. Click the Play Game button below to start the game.

Can you finish fastest with least moves possible ? Best of Luck !!
